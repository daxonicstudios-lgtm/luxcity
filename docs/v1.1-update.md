# LuxCity RP — v1.1 Update Roadmap

**Studio:** Daxonic Studios
**Current Version:** 1.0
**Target Version:** 1.1
**Engine:** Three.js (WebGL) — Single-file architecture (`index.html`)
**City:** 100% procedurally generated in code (no external city assets)

---

## Overview

v1.0 is a working open-world RP game with a procedurally generated city, driveable vehicle, animated character, and mobile-optimized touch controls. v1.1 focuses on **visual quality**, **city realism**, and **gameplay depth** — turning the prototype into something that looks and feels like a real city.

---

## 1. CITY ENVIRONMENT — Visual Overhaul

### 1.1 Trees (Priority: HIGH)

**Current state:** Trees are a green sphere on a brown cylinder — they look like cotton balls on sticks. Only 2 types exist (round tree + palm).

**Improvements:**
- Replace sphere leaves with **multi-layered cone geometry** (2-3 stacked cones decreasing in size) for a natural silhouette
- Add **tree species variety**:
  - **Oak** — wide, rounded canopy using merged spheres at different offsets
  - **Pine/Conifer** — 3 stacked cones (large base → small top), darker green
  - **Birch** — thin white trunk, smaller scattered leaf clusters
  - **Palm (improved)** — curved fronds using TubeGeometry or curved planes instead of flat rectangles
- **Randomization per tree**: height ±20%, trunk thickness ±15%, lean angle ±5°, leaf color variation (hue shift ±10%)
- Add **fallen leaves** as small scattered planes near tree bases
- Increase leaf segment count from 10 to 14-16 for smoother shapes

### 1.2 Buildings (Priority: HIGH)

**Current state:** Buildings are flat-colored boxes. Windows are single emissive strips per floor (not individual windows). No street-level detail.

**Improvements:**

**Skyscrapers:**
- Add **setbacks** — top section narrower than base (common in real skyscrapers)
- Individual **window grid** — small emissive rectangles with dark frames between them
- **Glass curtain wall** option — reflective material with MeshPhysicalMaterial (metalness: 0.9, roughness: 0.1)
- **Rooftop variety** — water towers, antenna arrays, helicopter pads, AC unit clusters
- Add **entrance canopy** at ground level with glass material

**Houses:**
- Add **front porch** geometry (small platform + roof overhang)
- **Fence/yard** — low box geometry fence around property, different grass shade inside
- **Garage doors** with panel lines (inset geometry)
- **Chimney** on some houses
- Varied **roof colors** (terra cotta, slate gray, dark brown)

**Commercial Buildings:**
- Add **shop awnings** at street level (half-cylinder geometry, colored)
- **Signage** — flat planes with emissive colored materials above doorways
- **Display windows** — larger glass panels at ground floor with interior lighting
- **Neon outlines** on some buildings using emissive edge geometry

**All Buildings:**
- Add **window air conditioning units** as small extruded boxes on random windows
- **Ledges/cornices** — thin horizontal strips every few floors
- **Shadow receiving** improvements — ensure all faces receive shadows properly

### 1.3 Roads (Priority: HIGH)

**Current state:** Roads are flat dark planes with only yellow center line dashes. No lane markings, crosswalks, or curbs.

**Improvements:**
- **White lane dividers** — dashed lines between lanes (each road should have 2-4 lanes)
- **Pedestrian crosswalks** at every intersection — white zebra stripes perpendicular to road
- **Raised curbs** — thin extruded geometry along road edges (0.15m high), gray color
- **Turn arrows** at intersections — flat white arrow geometry on road surface
- **Stop lines** — solid white line before each intersection
- **Parking spots** — parallel lines along some road edges
- **Road texture variation** — slight color variation, crack patterns using darker patches
- **Manhole covers** — dark circles at random road positions
- **Sidewalk detail** — lighter color squares/tiles pattern on sidewalks

### 1.4 Terrain & Landscape (Priority: MEDIUM)

**Current state:** Flat green plane extending 2000m. No elevation, no water, no parks.

**Improvements:**
- **City parks** — designated green areas within the grid with:
  - Park benches (box geometry seats with backrest)
  - Fountain (cylinder base + particle water effect or stacked cylinders)
  - Walking paths (lighter colored strips through grass)
  - Flower beds (small colored spheres in clusters)
- **Grass variation** — patchy darker/lighter green areas, dirt patches near heavy-traffic areas
- **Small pond/lake** — reflective blue plane with MeshPhysicalMaterial, surrounded by trees
- **Elevation** — subtle terrain bumps outside city center using noise-based displacement

### 1.5 Street Props & Furniture (Priority: MEDIUM)

**Current state:** Only traffic lights and street lamps exist. City feels empty.

**Add:**
- **Benches** — along sidewalks every 30-40m (box seat + backrest + legs)
- **Trash cans** — cylinder with dome top, dark green, near benches
- **Fire hydrants** — short red cylinder with caps, every 50m along roads
- **Bus stops** — glass shelter (transparent material) with bench inside, sign post
- **Mailboxes** — blue box on post, near intersections
- **Bollards** — short metal posts along sidewalk edges
- **Newspaper stands** — small box with angled top, yellow/red color
- **Planters** — box with green sphere bush on top, outside buildings
- **Dumpsters** — large green box behind commercial buildings
- **Phone booths** — glass box with light inside (retro city feel)
- **Billboards** — large elevated signs along major roads with emissive colored panels
- **Road signs** — stop signs, speed limit, street name signs at intersections

## 2. UI / HUD Improvements

### 2.1 Minimap (Priority: HIGH)

**Current state:** 90x90px circle showing colored dots for buildings. No roads visible, no labels.

**Improvements:**
- **Draw road grid** as thin gray lines on minimap
- **Building outlines** instead of just dots — show actual building footprint shape
- **Compass indicator** — N/S/E/W markers around minimap edge
- **Zoom controls** — pinch or +/- buttons to zoom minimap in/out
- **Player trail** — faint dotted line showing recent path
- Increase size to **110x110px** for better readability

### 2.2 Pause / Settings Menu (Priority: MEDIUM)

**Current state:** No pause menu exists. No way to adjust settings.

**Add:**
- **ESC key / pause button** opens overlay menu
- Menu options: Resume, Settings, Controls Help, Exit to Title
- **Settings panel**:
  - Graphics quality (Low / Medium / High)
  - Shadow toggle
  - View distance slider
  - Camera sensitivity slider
  - Sound volume (when audio is added)
- Pause should freeze game loop

### 2.3 HUD Polish (Priority: LOW)

- Add **damage flash** (red screen edge vignette) when health decreases
- **Cash earned** popup animation (+$100 floating text)
- **XP gained** bar animation with level-up effect
- **Speedometer** — arc/gauge style instead of just a number
- **Vehicle damage indicator** when collision occurs

## 3. LIGHTING & ATMOSPHERE

### 3.1 Day/Night Cycle (Priority: HIGH)

**Current state:** Static lighting — permanent daytime, flat blue sky.

**Improvements:**
- **Sun movement** — directional light rotates over time (configurable cycle: 5-10 min real time)
- **Sky gradient** — ShaderMaterial sky dome that shifts:
  - Dawn: orange/pink horizon, dark blue zenith
  - Day: light blue (current)
  - Dusk: orange/purple
  - Night: dark blue/black with star dots
- **Building windows** — emissive intensity increases at night (windows light up)
- **Street lamps** — turn on at dusk (increase emissive + add point lights)
- **Neon signs** — glow brightly at night on commercial buildings
- **Moon** — simple emissive sphere during night
- **Ambient light** shifts warm (day) → cool blue (night)

### 3.2 Sky & Clouds (Priority: MEDIUM)

- Replace flat background color with **gradient sky dome**
- Add **cloud layer** — translucent white planes at high altitude, slowly drifting
- **Sun/moon disc** visible in sky

### 3.3 Atmospheric Effects (Priority: LOW)

- **Ground fog** at dawn/dusk — low-lying transparent planes
- **Dust particles** — subtle floating particles in sunlight
- **Rain** effect (particle system) as a weather option
- **Puddles** on roads during/after rain (reflective patches)

## 4. GAMEPLAY

### 4.1 NPC Pedestrians (Priority: HIGH)

- Spawn **10-20 NPCs** walking on sidewalks
- Simple **waypoint pathfinding** — walk between random sidewalk points
- Use same character model, varied colors via material swap
- NPCs stop at crosswalks, walk along sidewalks

### 4.2 Sound (Priority: HIGH)

- **Ambient city sounds** — distant traffic hum, birds, wind
- **Footstep sounds** — timed to walk/run animation
- **Car engine** — pitch scales with speed
- **Punch impact** sound effect
- **UI sounds** — button clicks, menu open/close
- **Music** — lo-fi or synthwave background track (toggleable)

### 4.3 Vehicle Variety (Priority: MEDIUM)

- Add **2-3 more vehicle types** using procedural geometry (not GLB):
  - **Taxi** — yellow sedan, box-based
  - **Police car** — black/white with roof lights
  - **Truck/Van** — larger box vehicle
- Different handling per vehicle (speed, acceleration, turning radius)

### 4.4 Combat / Interaction (Priority: MEDIUM)

- Punch should **detect NPCs/objects** in range and trigger reaction
- **Health system** — NPCs can fight back, player takes damage
- **Destructible props** — trash cans, benches knock over on collision
- **Vehicle enter** from any nearby car (not just initial spawned cars)

### 4.5 Economy & Missions (Priority: LOW)

- **Cash pickups** scattered around city (glowing $ icons)
- **Basic missions** — "Go to location X" waypoint system with reward
- **Wanted system** — police chase if player causes trouble
- **Taxi missions** — pick up NPC, drive to destination

## 5. PERFORMANCE OPTIMIZATIONS

### 5.1 Instanced Geometry (Priority: HIGH)

**Current state:** Each tree, lamp, bollard is a separate mesh — ~200+ draw calls for props alone.

**Fix:**
- Use **InstancedMesh** for all repeated props (trees, lamps, fire hydrants, benches)
- One draw call per prop type instead of one per instance
- Expected: 200+ draw calls → ~10 draw calls for props

### 5.2 LOD System (Priority: MEDIUM)

- **Buildings**: Swap to simpler box geometry beyond 100m
- **Trees**: Swap to billboard sprites beyond 80m
- **Props**: Hide small props (benches, hydrants) beyond 60m

### 5.3 Geometry Merging (Priority: MEDIUM)

- Merge all static road geometry into single BufferGeometry
- Merge all sidewalk geometry
- Merge all curb geometry
- Reduces draw calls significantly

### 5.4 Shadow Improvements (Priority: LOW)

- Increase shadow map to **2048x2048** on desktop
- Add **cascaded shadow maps** for better quality at distance
- Enable shadows on mobile for medium/high quality settings
