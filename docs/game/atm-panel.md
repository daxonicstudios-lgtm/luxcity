## Components

- **Balance Display:** `#atmBal` вҖ” current bank balance
- **Amount Input:** `#atmAmount` вҖ” number input for transaction amount
- **Deposit Button:** `#atmDeposit` вҖ” transfer cash to bank
- **Withdraw Button:** `#atmWithdraw` вҖ” transfer bank to cash
- **Close Button:** `#atmClose`
