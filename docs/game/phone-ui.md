## Components

- **Status Bar:** "LUXCITY" label + `#phoneTime` clock
- **App Screen:** `#phoneScreen` вҖ” grid of phone app icons
- **Home Button:** `#phoneBackBtn` вҖ” returns to phone home screen or closes phone

## Phone Apps

- **BANK** вҖ” check balance, deposit/withdraw (includes `#phoneBankAmt` input)
- **CONTACTS** вҖ” NPC contact list
- **MESSAGES** вҖ” in-game messages
- **TAXI** вҖ” fast travel service
- **NEWS** вҖ” city news/events
- **SETTINGS** вҖ” phone settings
